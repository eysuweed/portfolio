Poem

What would a fool have to do to quench his happiness, than to bathe in glory of wrongness?

To flee the wrath of sadness and be finally free, albeit the wrongful means?

Or to stay desolated with thoughts of grief in mind while doing nothing in sorts?

To chase euphoria in a distraught way, in spite of painful repercussions?

Or to stay rotten for entitiy, until death grabs the poor soul away?

- Unknown
